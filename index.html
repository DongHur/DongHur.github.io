Welcome to Neuro 121

Goal: In this course, we will introduce students to the study of animal behavior using modern computational tools. Importantly, we aim to make this introduction as democratic as possible by allowing students to collect data easily at their own pace and place, analyze the data using open-source software and interpret the results in the context of prior scholarly literature that is easily accessible.

Prerequisites

There is no prerequisites for this course because everything will be taught from scratch in this course. However, having some programming experience will serve you well in this course. We will cover the basics of programming that you will need for the course and leave links for you to learn more.

Grading

The final grade is based on the following weighting of your course work:
10% Attendance
20% Problem Sets
30% Mini-Projects
